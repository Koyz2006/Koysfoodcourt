Cooking Method

Grilling

Grilling is a cooking method that involves cooking food on a metal grate over a heat source to create a smoky, charred flavor. Grills can use gas, electricity, charcoal, or wood. Grilling is a quick way to cook meat and vegetables.

Frying

Frying is a dry-heat cooking method that involves cooking food in fat. There are several types of frying methods, including deep-frying, pan-frying, saut√©ing, and stir-frying. The main difference between these methods is the amount of fat used.

Air Frying

Air frying is a cooking method that uses hot air to cook food, resulting in a crispy exterior and moist interior. Air fryers are similar to convection ovens and use a fan to circulate hot air around food in a basket.

Roasting

Roasting is a dry-heat cooking method that uses hot air to cook food evenly on all sides. It's a slow cooking process that can be done in an oven, with an open flame, or another heat source. Roasting is versatile and can be used for a variety of foods, including vegetables and meat.
